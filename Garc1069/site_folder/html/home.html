Where I'm From

Hello there! My name is Lucila, and I come from the sunny state of Acapulco, Mexico. My nickname is Lucy, and that's how I prefer to be called by my friends and family. I have been living in the United States for 25 years now, and I've had the chance to visit some beautiful states like Idaho, Texas, and California. Currently, I am a resident of Washington and a student at Edmonds College. I feel fortunate to be studying at this college

My Dream

Enrolling at Edmonds College was a turning point in my life, both academically and personally. I was the first in my family to complete high school through the GED diploma program offered in Spanish, which was a huge accomplishment. Completing the program impacted my family and friends by encouraging them to continue their education. Although it was a challenging experience, it was gratifying. I am grateful for the college's role in my personal growth.

Future

The college has been more than just an educational institution - it has been like a family to me. They have provided me with unwavering support, helping me improve my English language skills and assisting me in pursuing a degree in computer information systems. I am grateful for the guidance and encouragement that I have received from the faculty and staff at Edmonds College.
